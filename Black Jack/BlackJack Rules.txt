# BlackJack Game: card game also called as 21
# The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
# If the total goes over 21: called a bust and you lose immediately
# all cards from 2 to 10: count as face value
# Jack, Queen, King: count as 10
# Ace: count as 1 or 11

# Players can either stand or hit.
# Players can stand if they have a total of 17 or more.
# Players should hit if they have a total of 16 or less.
